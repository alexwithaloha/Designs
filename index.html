Aloha! I am Alex Shortsleeve.

I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in Travel Industry Management with a Concentration in Hospitality from the University of Hawaii, and an MBA from Hawaii Pacific University. I have also completed several post-graduate courses in Higher Education Leadership.

I have been a sales and marketing representative for several different food service companies. I have owned and operated separate businesses such as: a food brokerage firm and an event and meeting planning company. I have also assisted in the launch of a college entrance exam prep company.

I've taught classes at the undergraduate level for more than ten years covering a variety of topics which have included: Business Management, Sales & Marketing, Quality Service & Training, and Entrepreneurship.

I previously served as the Director of Education & Training for the Non-profit organization group IMCEA (International Military Community Executives' Association), and as the School Director for Kaplan International School of English.

I recently worked as a Consultant with the University of California San Diego and the University of California Irvine as an LMS Analyst/Administrator with their SumTotal LMS, and with Cymer Light Sources to develop their Cornerstone on Demand LMS. I am currently working with the University of Delaware in administering their Canvas LMS.
